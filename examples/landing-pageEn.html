The idea of the project

The main idea is the creation of a decentralized system for digital identification and authentication of users.


Secure


Based on blockchain technology (network and consensus level - on Tendermint BFT, application modules - on Cosmos-SDK).

Decentralized


Managed by verified users.

Public


Supported by interested servers owners which don't affect the content of the database, but only provide computing powers.

Learn more

The goal

The main gоаl is to except duplicate accounts, false identification, etc., by the means of malicious actions.

The system allows


• Invite users

• Carry out verification and guarantee the uniqueness of users

• Accumulate confirmation of the identity of various documents and user data

• Guarantee the validity and permanence data within the system

• Plug in various third-party services

• Motivate and reward validators

• Manage the rights of administrators

We guarantee


• Absolute owning and controlling their data

• Protection against unauthorized access to their data by using the acceptance mechanism

• Interaction with connected services through a mechanism for confirming their identity

• Transparency of the process of activation or deactivation of different services in the system and equal participation in endowing them with the function of system administration
